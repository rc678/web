Australian Open

This grand slam usually takes place during the end of January and is the first grand slam of the calendar year. This takes place in Melbourne,Australia. The game is played on a hard court. This game takes place at Melbourne Park which consists of the Rod Laver Arena, Hisense Arena and the Margaret Court Arena. Roy Emerson and Novak Djokovic are tied with the most singles titles at this tournament with 6 titles each.

French Open

This grand slam is the second one to take place during the year and starts in the middle of May. This grand slam is also known as the Roland Garros and takes place in Paris,France. The game is played on a clay court. This grand slam takes place at Stade Roland Garros which is named after the French aviator Roland Garros. Rafael Nadal won the most single titles of this grand slam by winning 9 times.

Wimbledon

This is the third grand slam to take place during the year. This is also known as The Championships and takes place in London,England. This grand slam is the only one in where the players have to wear all white since that is tradition.This grand slam is the only one to have strict dress codes. The game is played on a grass court. This is the oldest and most prestigious grand slam. It takes placein the All England Club. Roger Federer,William Renshaw and Pete Sampras are tied with the most single titles won at this tournament with 7 titles each.

US Open

This is the fourth and last grand slam to occur during the calendar year. This takes place in Flushing, NY. The game is played on a hard court at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center.Richard Sears, Bill Larned, and Bill Tilden each are tied for the msot singles titles won at this tournament with 7 titles each.
